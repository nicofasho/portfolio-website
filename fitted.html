Fitted

Project Overview

Fitted is a responsive exercise app that motivates users to lead a healthier lifestyle through easy to put together workout routines.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

Using the project brief given to me, I started my work with low fidelity wireframes to put some ideas I had about the different screens and flow of the app down on paper.

Moodboard

Alongside iterating wireframes which helped solidify the screens and flow of the app, I created a moodboard. Based on the project brief, this moodboard helped me keep the visual elements and tone of the app in line with its desired look and feel.

Updated Mockups

Here are some of the updated mockups combining the structure set through iterating wireframes and visual style of the moodboard. This responsive app was designed with a mobile-first approach, so that I could start with including only the most essential features and building up from there on larger screens.
